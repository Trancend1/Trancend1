## 🔭 Featured Project

#### [Weaver](https://github.com/Trancend1/weaver)

> A calmer way to translate and shape digital stories.

Weaver is a translation workbench built for people who work with long-form Japanese digital novels and want a more organized way to translate, review, and refine their work.

Instead of treating translation as a one-time AI output, Weaver helps break the process into a clearer flow: importing the story, keeping chapters structured, translating in smaller parts, reviewing the results, and keeping names, terms, and writing style consistent across the project.

The goal is simple: make novel translation feel less scattered, more manageable, and easier to polish from start to finish.

**What Weaver helps with**

* Importing digital novel files into a structured workspace
* Keeping volumes, chapters, and text segments organized
* Translating chapter by chapter or segment by segment
* Managing glossary terms and character names
* Reusing previous translation decisions for consistency
* Reviewing AI-generated translation candidates
* Checking for missing, inconsistent, or suspicious translations
* Preparing translated content for export
* Working locally without requiring accounts or telemetry
